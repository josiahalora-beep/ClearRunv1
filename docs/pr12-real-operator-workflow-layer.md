# PR12: Real Operator Workflow Layer

## Positioning

ClearRun is the pre-billing exception layer for FOG and liquid-waste route tickets.

It does not replace routing, dispatch, driver apps, invoicing, accounting, or customer service tools. It fits in the handoff after field work is completed but before the ticket is trusted for billing or customer proof.

## Core workflow

Job done → ticket returns → exception caught → follow-up sent → billing supported.

## Why it becomes worth paying for

Operators can manually inspect one ticket. That is not the product value.

The product value is turning manual ticket checking into a consistent exception queue:

- standard review rules
- aging exceptions
- owner assigned
- ready / hold logic
- dispatch follow-up
- billing visibility
- customer backup readiness

## Buyer pain

The real pain is not "records are disorganized." The real pain is completed work getting slowed down after the route because backup is weak.

Examples:

- Missing signed ticket
- No stop photo
- Gallons do not match the disposal ticket
- Customer asks for backup after billing
- Dispatch has to chase drivers after the job is over
- Billing does not know which tickets are safe to invoice

## Role-specific value

- Billing knows which tickets should wait before invoicing.
- Dispatch gets the exact driver/vendor follow-up to chase.
- Customer service has cleaner backup when an account asks for proof.
- Owners can see where completed work is getting stuck after service.

## Guardrails

- No fake customers
- No fake testimonials
- No compliance, certification, inspection-proof, or legal-sufficiency claims
- No claim that ClearRun replaces existing systems
- ClearRun stays focused on backup review and proof organization
