# PR09 Visual Foundation

## Reference direction

The uploaded Avio-style reference shows a premium editorial product site, not a generic SaaS page.

Key traits to carry into ClearRun:

- Lots of white space.
- Large restrained typography.
- Minimal copy per section.
- Black/white contrast used intentionally.
- Soft gradient product cards.
- Rounded premium modules.
- Clean top navigation.
- Product UI used as visual proof, not long explanation.
- Three strong cards instead of cluttered grids.
- Less footer heaviness.
- More breathing room between sections.

## ClearRun adaptation

ClearRun should not copy the healthcare content or purple-heavy palette. It should use:

- off-white background
- black / navy premium modules
- soft blue, sage, and muted grape gradients
- proof packet cards as the visual anchor
- concise closeout-readiness language
- utility-style product cards

## PR sequence

### PR09 — Visual Foundation

## Rules

- Do not add fake customers.
- Do not add fake testimonials.
- Do not claim compliance, certification, approval, or legal sufficiency.
- Avoid long paragraphs on customer-facing pages.
- Prefer concise product UI, cards, and visual hierarchy.
