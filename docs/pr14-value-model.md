# PR14 Exception Resolution Value Model

PR14 is designed around repeated operational work, not occasional proof-packet creation.

Illustrative model:

- 45 exceptions per month
- 8 minutes saved per exception
- 360 minutes saved
- 6 office hours saved
- $25 per office hour
- $150 modeled monthly value

This is the approximate break-even point for a $150/month add-on under the example assumptions.

The workflow must therefore reduce time across multiple steps:

- finding the blocker
- identifying the owner
- writing the follow-up
- tracking required proof
- deciding whether release conditions are satisfied
- preserving the activity trail

The model is illustrative and must never be presented as a promised customer result.
